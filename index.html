what we do

we reveal the unknown and chart a clear path to never before seen cost reductions

there is a reason no one has tried to do what we do: it can’t be done. Finding the absolute, most efficient solution is mathematically impossible. There are thousands of variables in your supply chain. Changing any one of them has implications that reverberate up and down the chain with consequences for all the other variables. Mathematicians call it an “NP Hard” problem, which means that with all the existing computer power on earth and all the known time in the universe the problem cannot be solved with a 100% degree of accuracy. For most, that would be a good reason to pursue other business models. Not us. We were stubborn enough to keep after the impossible. And in that, we discovered something revolutionary: while an absolutely perfect answer is unattainable, a 99% solution is not. And finding it uncovers millions of dollars in savings. We’re practical people. We will gladly trade the status and gratification of an academic solution for the real-world value of never-before-seen efficiencies for our clients.
